Fan-Game.
 
Combine Undertale to Fnaf in one game.

Sytle game fnaf-2

And apply 3d design.

Story
a story where Alphys, receiving a generous investment from the muffet, creates robots for the latter's establishment, Super Muffet buffet, the great establishment for children and adults, especially adults with their wallets.
Alphys creates super advanced robots with technology, magic and love, more advanced than Metaton, based on her friends, only quite changed to adapt to Muffet's needs, you are a rotten devil who must take care of the place, more than for the pay. by Muffet's hand, serving him almost like a slave.
Your task is to take care of the premises and make sure that nothing happens to it, regardless of whether your life is at risk.
Por amor a Muffet.

Game Style FNAF-2 combine mini games Undertale and Determination.

Pacifist route, fix things without destroying the animatronics and talk to Muffet.

Genocidal Route, kill all the animatronics and create an abomination to torture them, and burn the place.

Neutral Route, Destroy their self-esteem and leave them to avoid problems.
